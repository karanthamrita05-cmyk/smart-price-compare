About The Project

Problem Statement

Online shopping prices vary across platforms (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra...). Users waste time checking multiple sites. This project creates a unified prototype to compare product prices quickly.

Objectives

  • Build a user-friendly platform to compare product prices.
  • Help consumers save time and money.
  • Show future scope: integrate real APIs for live pricing.

SDG Relevance

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption — helps smart shopping and reduces overconsumption.
SDG 9: Industry & Innovation — digital tool that empowers consumers.
